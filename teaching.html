Creator, Remote Learning Series, Early Learning & Cognition Lab, UC San Diego
Initiated computational literacy series (online) after the UC San Diego campus shut down due to Covid-19. Taught Javascript, HTML, CSS, jsPsych, and GitHub so undergraduate and graduate students could learn how to transition their in-person testing to online platforms. Also taught design programs (e.g., Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Blender) to help colleagues create digital stimuli.

Creator, Research Workshop Series, Lab for the Developing Mind, New York University
Initiated workshop series to teach computational literacy and research design skills. Taught and invited NYU researchers to teach R statistical analyses, experimental design software (e.g., PsychoPy, PyHab), design programs (e.g., Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Blender). Also focused on research practices such as preregistration, reproducibility, and transparency.
